I'm an associate professor at the University of Ljubljana, Slovenia. I'm interested in spectral and scattering theory, orthogonal polynomials, complex analysis, Hankel and Toeplitz operators, and, more broadly, in the interplay of function and operator theories with applications in mathematical physics.

News

  • With A. Kostenko and U. Jezernik, we started the new Spectral Theory Seminar at the University of Ljubljana and IMFM.
  • I'm participating in the conference Spectral Theory and Mathematical Physics in honor of Barry Simon's 80th birthday.
